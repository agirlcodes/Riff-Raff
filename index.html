Info about studios
Riff Raff Studios is an indie game studio located in London, England, United Kingdom. We are a company that presents itself as friendly with a sense of humor, the term of riff raff meaning like undesirables or outcasts we hold that term to represent us making experimental games. We are a team of web and games designers and developers. We make our games to entertain and distract our gamers from their worries.
