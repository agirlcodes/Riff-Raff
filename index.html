Riff Raff Studios is an indie game studio located in London, England, United Kingdom. We are a company that presents itself as friendly with a sense of humor, using the term of riff raff, meaning like undesirables or outcasts we hold that term to represent us making experimental games.
We are a team of web and games designers and developers with a desire to produce our games to entertain and distract our gamers from their worries.
